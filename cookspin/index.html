Cook Spin

by GogoApps

Short Description

It is a simple tool to make quick recipes for those who can't decide what to cook for dinner.

Türkçe (TR)

Akşama ne yemek yapacağına karar veremeyenler için hızlı bir menü oluşturma aracı.

Full Disclosure

A simple app for those who can't decide what to cook for dinner. It consists of 1 button and 4 slots. Pressing the button suggests you 4 dishes. Clicking on the food names directs it to the search engine for the recipe. That's all!

Türkçe (TR)

Akşama ne yemek yapacağına karar veremeyenler için basit bir uygulama. 1 buton ve 4 slottan oluşuyor. Butona basıldığında size 4 kap yemek tavsiye ediyor. Yemek isimlerine tıklayınca da tarifi için arama motoruna yönlendiriyor. That's all!

Privacy Policy

Terms of Service

What this app will do with user data?

App only requires Gmail account for authenticated use. With this authentication users can use application personally. They can save their favorite recipes. That's for all.

How this app enhances user functionality?

Users can search through recipe lists. They can save their favorite recipes. Then they can draw a dinner menu within favorite recipes.
